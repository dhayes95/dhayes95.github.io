Interests

My current research is in tensor decompositions for both black-box approximations and the simulation of partial differential equations. I focus primarily on implementations of memory-efficient decompositions in various tensor settings to make large-scale computation feasible. I also have interest in the use of data-centric implementations that require little access into data sets in compressing information. Key words: Tensor-Train, Tucker, Hierarchical Tucker, Cross approximations, parallel computing.
